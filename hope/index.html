About Us

At Hope in a Bag, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn and grow. Our mission is to provide orphans and vulnerable children across Kenya with the educational tools they need to build a brighter future.

Through your generous donations, we supply school bags filled with essential items: notebooks, pens, pencils, textbooks, and other learning materials. Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty, and with your support, we can ensure that no child is left behind. Join us in making a difference— one bag at a time.

Our Programs

We run five core programs designed to uplift and empower children in our communities.
